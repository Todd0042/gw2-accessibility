# Accessibility Feature Suggestions

## Audio/TTS expansions
- **Skill cooldown alerts** — TTS when dodge/heal/utility come off cooldown
- **Food/utility expiry warnings** — "Food expired" or "Utility expired" when buffs expire
- **Squad commander ping TTS** — read commander pings/marker calls
- **Boss phase announcements** — "Phase 2 starting", "Enrage in 10 seconds"
- **Priority interrupt system** — high-priority mechanics interrupt lower-priority TTS
- **Per-channel voice settings** — different voice/pitch for mechanics vs chat vs debuffs

## Visual overlays
- **Screen flash/color overlay** for critical incoming mechanics (e.g., red flash for "Corruption")
- **Large text callout overlay** — big centered text for urgent mechanics instead of just TTS
- **Countdown timer overlay** — visual countdown for timed mechanics (greens, stacks, etc.)
- **Color-blind indicators** — pattern/shape overlays instead of relying on color

## Motor/mobility
- **Auto-interact** — automatically interact with objects when in range (chests, portals, etc.)
- **Quick-respond keybinds** — one-key responses ("I'm here", "Need healing", "Pulling") broadcast to squad
- **Simplified targeting** — auto-target nearest enemy / specific target type
- **Hold-to-cast** — hold a key to channel skills instead of clicking

## Cognitive
- **Step-by-step mechanic instructions** — instead of just "Corruption", speak "Corruption — get to fountain"
- **Encounter phase memory aids** — "Stack at boss", "Spread now", "Breakbar"
- **Simplified UI mode** — hide non-essential UI elements during combat

## Combat awareness
- **Incoming damage alerts** — TTS when taking heavy hits or specific damage types
- **Ally downed/death alerts** — "Player X is downed"
- **Buff stack counters** — TTS when reaching important boon/condition thresholds
